iPad in Education

iPad has changed Everything. Including Classroom.

Apps

iPad as teaching and learning means is changing everything. Including the classroom. It has changed the way teachers teach and taking learning way beyond the classroom. There are thousands of education apps, books and courses, to make lessons come to life like never before. The iPad has software designed exclusively for educators, to manage the classroom and collaborate with students in new ways.

Everyone can code

When you teach code, you’re not only teaching the language of technology. You’re teaching new ways to think and bring ideas to life. The Everyone Can Code curriculum will guide you through Swift Playgrounds, an app that teaches coding for kids through a world of interactive puzzles and playful characters. It’s great for students aged 8 and up, and will help you introduce coding in the same language used by professional app developers.

Books

The iBooks store is full of interactive materials to support teacher source the detailed knowledge about any topic of any subject so that teacher has complete knowledge to quench the thirst of student. An engaging and interactive experience is offered to students by the Multi-Touch magic of iBooks Textbooks.

iTunes U for iPad

With iTunes U, it’s easy to develop and deliver lessons on iPad that include apps, web links, photos and videos. With an integrated grade book and access to the world’s largest library of free educational content, you’ll find inspiring materials to incorporate into any lesson plan.

Classroom App

This is a powerful free iPad app that helps teachers guide learning, share work and manage student devices. It is easy to set up on the teacher’s iPad with the help of MDM.

Shared iPad

This provides a personalised learning experience using shared devices, even when a 1:1 student-to-iPad ratio isn’t possible. This also provides opportunity for collaborative learning such as group project, etc. After students log in, their homework, apps and assignments appear exactly as they left them, regardless of which iPad they last used.

Great device for Digital Classroom
