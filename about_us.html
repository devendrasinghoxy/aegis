About Us

Established in 2000,  Aegis Infotech Pvt Ltd is Central India’s leading company in the IT sphere, providing latest software, quality hardware, and IT solutions to almost all major educational institutions and corporates in Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Rajasthan and Chhattisgarh.

We are a part of TRK Group, that has diverse business interest in the areas of reality, power quality diagnostics and management solution through 50-50 JV with Israel headquartered global leader in the field.

Our strength is our a professional workforce that includes Microsoft and Apple certified technical executives and trainers. Our technically competent and skilled manpower is backed by the finest infrastructure facilities. We operate in a corporate environment with total workforce of over 40 executives across all group companies.
